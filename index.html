Sync Your Photos

Sync your photos to a static site on the web, with long term storage of originals

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

You have a ton of photos on your laptop or desktop. You never take photos off of your phone. You have a number of increasingly larger hard drives that you backup your originals to.

Sync Your Photos tries to make it as simple as possible for you to have all of your photos online in one spot, as well as have a long term archive of the originals.
